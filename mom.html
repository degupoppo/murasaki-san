Auction Rules 📘

  • Memento of Murasaki-san (MoM) is a full-on-chain NFT. Due to its SVG format, it may not display correctly in certain wallets, such as MetaMask.
  • NFTs will be minted in the Nouns DAO format auction mechanism. Minting and revealing occur at the end of the auction (they are not minted at the acution start).
  • The auction operates in 72-hour cycles, commencing with a minimum price of 10 $ASTR. If bidding takes place within the last hour of the auction, it extends by an additional hour.
  • The minimum bidding price is set at an amount that is 5% higher than the current bidding price until 24 hours before the end of the auction, and the interest rate increases as the auction closing approaches. Within 24 hours before the auction ending, it is set at 6%; within 12 hours, it is 6.5%; within 6 hours, it is 7.5%; and within 1 hour, it is set at 10%.
  • At the bidding, the bidder can edit the text to be displayed in the NFT's outer frame. At the end of the auction, the text set by the winner is written into the NFT. (Text rules: Less than 50 characters, can use [0-9], [a-z], [A-Z], [space], [#;&@.,!?-]).
  • If the wallet owning Murasaki-san SBT wins the auction, a bonus presentbox will be delivered to their House of Murasaki-san.
  • 100% of the auction proceeds are transferred to the Vault of House of Murasaki-san project, where they will be utilized to expand their ecosystem.


NFT Details 📗

  • Contract address of NFT:
    0x20f18BeDd45a6d6631D3a92ac501d03a51Ac9D18
  • The artwork in the NFT is a programmable picture that combines PNG data and SVG code. By coordinating multiple contracts, SVG-format pictures are generated as tokenURI. All data is held within the contract, with no off-chain data included.
  • NFTs are minted by combining 6 types of characters, 12 background colors, 12 types of fluffy, 5 types of pippel, 6 types of floral decorations, and over 20 different flavor texts displayed in the NFT's description field. Each element may be subject to future additions and modifications.
  • The selection probability of each element in an NFT is the same, and there is no difference in NFT rarity (no rare pictures or colors).
  • The pippel within the NFT artwork grows slowly over time, and the size of the flower increases as time passes. It reaches its maximum growth in approximately six months.
  • There is a hidden Astar logo within the NFT artwork. Try to find it :)
  • MoM is an ERC721 standard NFT, and owners can freely buy, sell, and transfer it in any marketplace. There is no transfer fee set.
