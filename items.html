Item Details

All items are minted as NFTs (ERC-721),
and they take effect when held in your wallet.

Craftable Items

In House of Murasaki-san, there exist 48 types of NFTs that Murasaki-san can craft. By minting each NFT and holding it in your wallet, an item will be installed on your house, and the corresponding status of your Murasaki-san will be boosted. The degree of status boost varies depending on the rarity of the NFT (common: +1.0, uncommon: +1.5, rare: +2.0). Even if you possess multiple copies of the same NFT, the status value will only increase slightly (the status increase of the second NFT is 1/10). It's advisable to possess various NFTs evenly. Whether an NFT can be crafted in the early or late game, the degree of status increase remains the same.

Other Items

In addition to the regular craftable items, several types of items are available as NFTs. The Coin Bank and Leaf Pouch are convenient for exchanging coins and leaves with other players. If you already have a Cat Cushion in your house, you can craft Cat Mail to send to other players. The Presentbox may be received as a reward at various times, and when opened, it releases a random fluffy.

Fluffies

Fluffy is a small housemate in your house who dreams of becomming Murasaki-san. The more fluffies that live in the house, the more luck your Murasaki-san will have. You can upgrade your fluffy from a lower level to an upper level by burning fluffies of the same color. There are a total of 12 colors available.
